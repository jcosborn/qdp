Adjoint

The adjoint of an operand is specified by a suffix a after the type abbreviation. Thus

QDP_V_eq_Ma_times_V(c, u, b, s);

carries out the product

c[x] = adjoint(u[x])*b[x];

for all sites x in subset s.
